Privacy, Terms & Payments

Last updated 23 July 2026. Plain-language explanation of how IITH Sanchari works, what happens to your data, and how payments are handled.

01What this is

IITH Sanchari is a ticket booking tool for campus shuttle and outstation buses used by the IIT Hyderabad community, developed in coordination with the IIT Hyderabad Transport Department to make bus travel booking faster and easier.

02Privacy — what we collect

We collect the minimum needed to issue a valid ticket, and nothing else:

  • Name and phone number, entered at the time of purchase — used only to identify you as the ticket holder to the driver.
  • No cookies, analytics, or trackers are set by this app. You can verify this yourself — the site is static HTML/CSS/JS with no third-party scripts other than the Razorpay checkout described below.
  • Standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, timestamp) are kept temporarily by our hosting provider, as with any website — we don't actively use or analyze these.

We never see, store, or have access to your card number, UPI PIN, or bank details — those go directly to Razorpay (see below).

03Payments

All payments are processed by Razorpay, a licensed payment aggregator regulated by the Reserve Bank of India. When you tap Buy:

  • The fare is fixed by our server based on the route — never something you or your browser can alter.
  • Razorpay's own secure checkout opens to collect payment details. This app never touches your card, UPI, or banking information at any point.
  • Razorpay confirms payment directly to our server before a ticket is considered valid.

Razorpay's handling of your payment data is governed by Razorpay's own Privacy Policy.

04Refunds & cancellations

Tickets are non-refundable once issued, except where the trip is cancelled or a ticket fails to work through a fault of this app or the operator — in that case, contact us (below) for a refund.

Fares, timings, and routes shown in the app reflect the best available information from operators and are subject to change without notice.

05Terms of use

  • This app is provided as a convenience for booking; it does not operate the buses themselves. Delays, cancellations, or service issues caused by transport operators are outside this app's control.
  • You're responsible for carrying valid ID and arriving at the boarding point on time, per each route's terms shown in the app.
  • We reserve the right to update these terms, the app, or its features at any time.
